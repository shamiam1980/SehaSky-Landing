Our Services

Personal Health Record (PHR)

The PHR is your personal record. It"s a place where you can store your own health information (such as allergies, conditions, medications, documents, and measurements) and import information from various sources in order to create a record which is as complete as possible. If you connect to another source of health data (for example, your GP system using the GP access feature), we will copy information from that source into your PHR so that it is always available to you. Your PHR is your data, you own it and you control it. We will not give or sell it to anyone else unless you tell us to.

Check our Privacy Policy for more details.

They will also be able to add entries into your PHR. If you decide to use the Trusted Access feature, you acknowledge the following:

  • We cannot and do not take any responsibility for what the other person will do with your information, for example:
    1. The other person may use their own means to download, save or store your record;
