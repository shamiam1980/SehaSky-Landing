About Us

Who are us?

SehaSky is an online medical platform where patients can connect with doctors in a smooth cooperative manner. Each patient will have a medical record that encompasses all aspects of current and past health status. These records will be accessible by doctors to reach an accurate diagnosis. With this data, we can direct each person to the right doctor and create a customized plan for every person towards a perfectly healthy life. Our users can also receive informative content produced by doctors giving advices on how to avoid certain health issues, correcting popular misperceptions and myths or educating the public about a specific subject.

In SehaSky, doctors and patients are closer than ever.

Why us?

You may also give us information, and we may collect and process information about you resulting from, any interactions you undertake or services you request or source from us.

You are a Patient

In SehaSky every doctor is your personal doctor. Every doctor is a family member you often seek help from, a family member who knows you very well and understands your unique needs. But that's not all right? Another common problem is prescriptions that can't be taken in parallel which can unfortunately sometimes lead to serious problems, probably more serious than the reason for which you went to the doctor in the first place.

Yet, through uniting many doctors of different specialties under SehaSky umbrella, prescriptions will never clash again. A unified prescription is produced based on medical history and current treatment that can be accessed by all doctors within our network.

When you register with us, you create a medical record that contains your current and past health information. By doing so, your case becomes as coherent as possible to any doctor, reducing chances of medical errors, inaccurate diagnosis, or adverse effects.

One other crucial service we provide is that we direct you to the right doctor of appropriate specialty based on your needs. Whenever you don't feel well, all you got to do is call us, one of our general physicians will answer and direct you to the right doctor for your symptoms.

In addition to all of that, all members registered in our network will have access to many exclusive posts and articles written by none other than your favorite doctors on SehaSky.
